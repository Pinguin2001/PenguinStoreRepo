Description

This app will allow you to stream your camera (Built-in or USB) to local web link.
Usage:
After installing the app and launch
Go to any browser and enter the URL:
http://yourDeviceIP:portNumber
Like: http://192.168.0.111:8000
Enhanced by Bashar Astifan. For more options and info (Including privacy concerns)
Checkout GitHub Page:
